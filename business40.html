Opportunity for Vietnam's Bakery Industry to Shine at VIBS 2024

9:23:43 AM | 9/11/2025

The International Bakery Show (VIBS 2024) take place from December 11 to 14, 2024, at the Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC), District 7, Ho Chi Minh City.


The event is organized by Yorkers Exhibition Service Vietnam Co., Ltd. in collaboration with the Taipei Bakery Association (TBA). This year's exhibition marks a grand return after its inaugural event in 2019, with VIBS 2024 featuring nearly 200 booths from over 120 prestigious exhibitors representing both domestic and international brands. The exhibition promises to provide opportunities for trade and business promotion among bakery enterprises both locally and abroad. Notably, it will also serve as a platform for talented and professional bakers from Vietnam and around the world to showcase their most creative skills in baking competitions held during the exhibition.


Potential of the Vietnamese bakery market


Vietnam is regarded as a large and promising market for the bakery industry, which is expected to grow significantly in the near future. Therefore, the search for new solutions and technologies to enhance competitiveness and diversify products, as well as attract consumers, is a key focus for businesses in this sector.


TBA has noted that the bakery industry, particularly the pastry segment in Vietnam, is recognized as one of the fastest-growing sectors and the most competitive segment within the consumer goods industry. With the advantage of a large and youthful population, Vietnam is considered one of the most promising bakery markets in Southeast Asia.

VIBS 2024 will feature a wide range of diverse products, from modern baking machinery and high-quality ingredients to finished baked goods. Attendees will have the opportunity to experience a comprehensive view of the rapidly growing Vietnamese bakery industry, along with quality products from the international market.

VIBS 2024 attracts significant interest from the global bakery community, especially representatives from countries and territories that are members of the International Federation of Chinese Pastry Associations (IFCBCA), including the Philippines, Singapore, Malaysia, Myanmar, China, Taiwan, and Hong Kong, along with representatives from the world's leading bakery exhibition organizing unit.
